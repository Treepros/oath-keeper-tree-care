FAQs AND ANSWERS

What factors determine the cost of tree removal?
The cost of tree removal depends on several factors, including the size and height of the tree, its location and accessibility, the complexity of the job, and any additional services required, such as stump grinding or debris removal.
How often should trees be trimmed?
Trees should typically be trimmed every 3-5 years, depending on the type and growth rate of the tree. Regular trimming helps maintain the tree's health, shape, and safety by removing dead or diseased branches.
Is tree removal covered by insurance?
Tree removal is sometimes covered by homeowner's insurance, particularly if the tree poses a danger to structures or has caused damage due to a storm. It's best to check with your insurance provider to understand your specific coverage.
What is the best time of year to perform tree trimming?
The best time to trim most trees is during the late winter or early spring, when the tree is dormant. This minimizes stress on the tree and allows for more precise pruning before new growth begins.
What should I look for in a tree service company?
When choosing a tree service company, look for a licensed and insured provider with a good reputation and positive reviews. It's also important that they have experienced and certified arborists, use proper safety equipment, and provide a detailed estimate before starting the work.
